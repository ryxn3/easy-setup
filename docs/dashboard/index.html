"Online right now" counts open tabs on the main site (incremented on load, decremented when the tab closes) — it's a best-effort live count, not a certified analytics number: a crashed tab or force-quit browser can occasionally leave it a little high until it naturally settles. "Visits all-time" counts page loads, not unique people, so refreshes count again. Powered by counterapi.dev, a free anonymous counter — nothing about you is tracked or stored.
